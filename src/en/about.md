# About

Navaid Space is a space for mutual support and collaboration.

## Meaning of the Name

The name "Navaid Space" is derived from the word "navaid," which means "navigator" or "voyager."
Therefore, Navaid Space can be simply understood as: a space that can be planned and guided for the future through mutual assistance.

- The Future
